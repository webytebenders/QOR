Fee Economics

Where the 1% Goes

Every basis point has a job. Transparent, on-chain, verifiable.

Junction Fees (Relayer Nodes) 0.6%

Paid in USDC to relayer node operators. Incentivizes infrastructure providers to bond $QOR and run the network.
