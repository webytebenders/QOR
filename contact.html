Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about QOR, biometric wallets, and the ecosystem.

QOR uses your device's Secure Enclave (or equivalent hardware security module) to generate and store private keys. Your biometric data (Face ID, Touch ID) authenticates you locally on-device. The biometric template never leaves your phone — it's processed entirely within the hardware chip. QOR never sees, stores, or transmits your biometric data.

QOR provides multiple recovery options: (1) Multi-device sync — register your biometrics on a secondary device before your primary is lost. (2) Social recovery — trusted contacts can initiate a time-delayed recovery process. (3) Dead Man's Switch — if no check-in occurs within your defined window, funds auto-transfer to your designated beneficiaries. No seed phrase needed for any recovery path.
