Privacy Policy

How QOR protects your data, your identity, and your sovereignty.

Last updated: March 2026

At QOR, privacy is not a feature — it is a foundational principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the QOR website, application, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the Service. This may include:

  • Email address (when you sign up for early access or contact us)
  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility and support)
  • Usage analytics (aggregated, non-personally-identifiable interaction data)
  • Wallet address (generated locally on your device)

2. Biometric Data

QOR uses biometric authentication (such as Face ID or Touch ID) to secure your wallet and authorize transactions. It is critical to understand the following:

  • Your biometric data NEVER leaves your device. It is processed and stored exclusively within your device's Secure Enclave hardware chip.
  • QOR does not collect, transmit, store, or have access to your biometric templates at any time.
  • Biometric verification is handled entirely by your device's native operating system security framework (e.g., Apple's Secure Enclave, Android's StrongBox).
  • No biometric data is ever sent to QOR servers, third-party services, or the blockchain.

This architecture ensures that your biometric identity remains under your sole control at all times — true sovereign custody of both your assets and your identity.

3. Personal Information

We do not require personal identification documents, social security numbers, or government-issued IDs to use the core QOR wallet. If you voluntarily opt into institutional compliance features (such as ZK-powered KYC), any identity verification is processed through zero-knowledge proofs — meaning the institution receives mathematical proof of your identity without ever seeing your raw personal data.

4. How We Use Your Data

Any information we do collect is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and early access
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect any data we do handle:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Hardware-level key storage via Secure Enclave technology
  • Zero-knowledge architecture that minimizes data exposure by design
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

While no system is 100% immune to risk, QOR's privacy-first architecture ensures that even in the unlikely event of a breach, there is no centralized repository of biometric data or private keys to compromise.

6. Third Parties

We may use a limited number of third-party services for analytics, infrastructure, and communication. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and are prohibited from using it for any purpose other than providing their services to QOR. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Cookies & Tracking

The QOR website may use essential cookies to ensure proper functionality (such as session management and security). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, though some features of the website may not function as intended.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

hello@qor.io

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
