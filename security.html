Security Architecture

Defense in Depth.
By Design.

Enterprise-grade physical and digital security — from the Secure Enclave chip to zero-knowledge compliance. No compromises.

Multi-Layer Defense

Four Layers of Protection

Every layer is independent. Even if one is compromised, your assets remain safe.

Layer 1

Secure Enclave

Private keys are generated and stored inside your device's hardware security chip (SecP256R1). They never leave the chip — not to the OS, not to the cloud, not to QOR. Physical hardware isolation.

Layer 2

Biometric Auth

Face ID, Touch ID, or device-native biometrics. Your biological identity is the only key. Biometric templates are processed on-device — never uploaded, never shared, never stored externally.

Layer 3

On-Chain Verification

Solana's SIMD-0075 enables native biometric signature verification at the protocol level. Not a wrapper, not a proxy — real cryptographic verification baked into the blockchain itself.

Layer 4

Zero-Knowledge Proofs

When compliance requires identity verification, ZK proofs let institutions confirm your identity without ever accessing your data. Only mathematical proof is published — never personal information.

Physical Security

Defense Against Physical Coercion

The $5 wrench attack is real. QOR is the only protocol designed to defend against it.

Scenario A

Remote Hack

Vault Mode

48-hour timelock on large transfers. Even if an attacker gains access, funds can't move instantly. User executes "Biological Veto" to cancel the attack during the waiting period.

Session Limits

Configurable per-session transaction caps. Even a compromised session can only move pre-defined amounts.

Scenario B

Coercion / $5 Wrench

Duress PIN

A secondary biometric scan or PIN opens a "Decoy PDA" with minimal funds. The attacker sees a real-looking wallet with a small balance — never knowing the real vault exists.

Decoy Finger Scan

Register a secondary finger as the duress trigger. Under coercion, the wrong finger opens the decoy. The attacker has no way to know.

Scenario C

Incapacitation

Dead Man's Switch

If no biometric check-in occurs within a user-defined window (e.g. 90 days), funds are automatically transferred to pre-designated beneficiary wallets. Probate-proof estate planning.

Multi-Sig Recovery

Optionally designate trusted contacts who can initiate recovery through a multi-signature process with time delays.

Hardware Security

Inside the Secure Enclave

Your private key lives in a physically isolated chip inside your phone. It cannot be extracted — not by QOR, not by Apple, not by anyone. Here's how it works.

01

Key Generation

A SecP256R1 keypair is generated inside the Secure Enclave. The private key is created and stored in hardware — it never exists in software memory.

02

Biometric Binding

The key is bound to your biometric identity. Only your authenticated face or fingerprint can authorize the Secure Enclave to sign transactions.

03

Sign & Broadcast

When you transact, the Secure Enclave signs the payload internally and outputs only the signature. The private key never leaves the chip.
