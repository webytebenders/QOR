Six Pillars

Built for the Future of Finance

A complete sovereign financial architecture — every piece designed to work together seamlessly.

Biometric Wallet

Your face or fingerprint is your private key. The wallet is generated inside your device's Secure Enclave hardware chip — biometric templates never leave the device. No seed phrases, no passwords, no cloud storage. Just scan and access.

  • 5-second onboarding
  • SecP256R1 hardware security
  • Face ID / Touch ID native

P2P Marketplace

Trade directly with others — no centralized exchange, no middleman, no custody risk. Biometric-authenticated escrow ensures both parties are verified before funds move. True peer-to-peer commerce.

  • Biometric escrow
  • Zero custody risk
  • Instant settlement

Staking & Rewards

Real yield from merchant processing fees — not printed inflation. When merchants process payments through QOR, 1% flows into automatic buyback & burn. Stakers earn from actual economic activity.

  • Merchant fee-powered yield
  • Automatic buyback & burn
  • Deflationary by design

ZK Compliance

Zero-knowledge proofs let institutions verify your identity without seeing your data. Rich personal data goes in, only a mathematical proof comes out. Privacy preserved, compliance achieved.

  • 128-byte Merkle root output
  • ISO-standard identity reports
  • Real-time sanctions screening

EVM Escape Hatch

QOR's cross-chain Octane layer extends biometric UX to Ethereum, Base, BSC, and any EVM chain. Users aren't locked in — they get sovereign access to the entire multi-chain ecosystem.

  • Ethereum, Base, BSC support
  • Biometric UX on any chain
  • Chain-agnostic by design

Bonding Network

Relayer nodes lock 100,000 $QOR to operate. More transaction volume means more demand for bonded nodes, driving buy pressure. 20% of total supply permanently locked by infrastructure providers.

  • 100K QOR lock per node
  • 20% supply permanently bonded
  • Self-reinforcing flywheel
